 Dyaas, beʼelaʼeʼ yàan k kanik tùumben tʼaanoʼob.
 Máakalmáak tʼàanoʼob?
 Teneʼ in wòohel hun pʼéel túumben, “huhun pʼíitil”
 Laura, aʼal “huhun pʼíitil”. Aʼaleh!
 “Huhun pʼíitil”
 Felipe, tsʼíibt “huhun pʼíitil”. Tsʼíibteh!
 Haah, Felipe máanen a tsʼíibteh. Tsʼíibt le tʼàanoʼ.
 Bix a waʼalik Laura?
 Máanen!
 Maʼalob, yàan in máan.
 Laura, xokeh. Xok le tsʼíiboʼ.
 Uʼuy in xòok Felipe!
 Maʼalob, xokeh.
 Maʼalob a xòokeʼex yéetel a tsʼíibeʼex. Chéen beyoʼ, tsʼoʼokih!
