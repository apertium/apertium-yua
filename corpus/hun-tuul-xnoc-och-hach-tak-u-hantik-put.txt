Huntul x-nukoch hach taku hantik put
tsikbaltabi tumen Martina Yu Chan, Tikul
Yan bin huntul x-nukoche, seten uts tu than u hantiku takni put. ka bini ich kol, ka
mani tuux yan humpʼel chum put, chinchinkbal u takni. ka hopʼ u naakale, pero
kaanal tun, ma tu paahtal u kuchli. Bey ku betik, ka man tulakal le akabe, ma kuchli
tuux yan le puto utial u emse, utial u hante. Naakal u yol yetel ma tu paahtal u
emsike. ka tu yilah tu sastale ka tu yalah beya, "Humf. Yax chune, ma takin hantic
le puto. Mix takni."
